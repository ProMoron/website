Notes

This piece was inspired by a television program i was watching at my aunts house. It was about Stuart the explorer's journey through the northern territory, charting a course for the telegraph lines. I saw the desolate grassy fields and the beautiful mountains and knew I had to draw them. Taking inspration from dot techniques I placed complimentary and contrasting dots over the flat colours representing fields, rocks, a lake and the mountains. The finishing touch is the sky complete with some messy stars that float down over the landscape.

Materials

  • Standard A4 notebook paper
  • POSCA acryllic paint pens

Techniques

  • Dot Art
  • Finger Painting
